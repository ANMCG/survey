Welcome to Our Survey

Thank you for your interest in our survey! In this survey, we study your evaluation of artificial music. We have used different methods to create artificial melodies. To know which method works best, we need your opinion on the melodies. Participating in this survey takes about XX minutes.

In this survey,

You will compare pairs of melodies. For each pair, you have to indicate which one you prefer, and which one sounds most natural to you. After you have given your preferences, the next pair of melodies will be played. If, for some reason, you did not hear the melodies correctly, you can press "play buttons" to listen to them again. However, you do not have to listen to melodies multiple times. Just go for your gut feelings when evaluating them.

Press the "play button" above to listen to an example melody. All melodies will be as loud as this one. Please check whether you can hear the melody and change your speaker loudness accordingly. You can play the melody multiple times.
