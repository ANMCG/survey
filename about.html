Who are we?

We are Mustafa Erkan Basar, Diede Kemper, Danny Merkx and Robin Wellner. We are doing the Artificial Intelligence Master program at the Radboud University of Nijmegen, The Netherlands. This survey is part of a research project for the course Artificial and Natural Music Cognition, which is instructed by Makiko Sadakata, Peter Desain and Jana Krutwig.
